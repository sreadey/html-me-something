Still Crazy

In the summer of 1960, two young kids met and became fast friends. Over the years they played baseball, hiked in the woods, waded through streams, body-surfed at Scarborough Beach, built forts, had snowball fights, went ice-skating and sledding. After High School, life took them in different directions, but, in the Summer of 2012, they reunited. Due to a mutual love of the great outdoors and always up for a good physical challenge, one of them said "Let's do a Mudder"...and so they did!

Tough Mudder - July 15, 2012

Over 10 miles running, (sometimes walking) up and down the rugged flanks of Mt.Snow with more than 20 military-style obstacles, including:

  • Artic Enema (ice filled dumpster diving)
  • Berlin Walls (scalling 10' sheer walls)
  • Funky Monkey (traverse a pond by way of monkey bars)
  • Electroshock Therapy (10,000 volts!)
